A total of eight blend files with the 3D reconstructions of axons, synaptic vesicles and mitochondria were generated. Two are under control conditions, and two are under LTP. The files named FPNCT and XRZCT are control and LTP conditions, respectively, from animal JB023. The files named FHLTD and FWNGV are control and LTP conditions, respectively, from animal JB024. 

Additionally, four files were generated for APV treatment. Files named LKJKD and CVZWG are under control and LTP conditions, from animal JB064, while files named JYBWM and JPTZX correspond to  control and LTP conditions, from animal JB056.

In the blend files, axons are named ax_XX, where XX is a number, the synaptic vesicles cluster is named as *_ssvr, mitochondria are name *_mito.

Measurements were performed on the blend files and recorded in both Excel and a CSV files. The cvs file was used to generate the figures.
